Protect Your Account

Enhance your security by enabling Two-Factor Authentication. Follow the steps below to link your authenticator app.

Step 1: Scan QR Code

Open an authenticator app (like Google Authenticator, Authy, or Microsoft Authenticator) and scan this QR code.

Can't scan? Use setup key

Step 2: Enter 6-digit Code

Enter the 6-digit code generated by your app to verify the setup.
