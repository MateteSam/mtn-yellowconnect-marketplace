Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 24, 2023

At MTN Marketplace, your privacy is our priority. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our platform, ensuring high-trust innovation and localized relevance in every interaction. We are committed to transparency and empowering you with control over your digital footprint.

We collect information to provide better, highly relevant services to all our users—from basic account setup to advanced AI-driven recommendations. Types of data include:

  • Account Information: Name, phone number, email address, and business details.
  • Transaction Data: Purchase history, payment methods, and shipping addresses.
  • Device & Usage Data: IP addresses, browser types, and interaction metrics with our platform.

Your data powers the intelligence of the MTN Marketplace. We utilize it to:

  • Process secure transactions rapidly.
  • Enhance localized search results and personalize product recommendations.
  • Detect, prevent, and address fraud, technical issues, or security breaches.
  • Communicate important service updates and localized promotional offers.

We do not sell your personal information. We only share data under strict confidentiality agreements with:

  • Trusted Service Providers: Partners who assist in payment processing, logistics, and customer support.
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law to comply with valid legal processes or protect the rights, property, or safety of MTN and its users.

You maintain ultimate control over your data within the MTN ecosystem. You have the right to:

  • Access and review the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements).
  • Opt-out of non-essential marketing communications at any time.

Have Privacy Concerns?

Our dedicated Data Protection team is ready to assist you with any inquiries regarding your personal information.
