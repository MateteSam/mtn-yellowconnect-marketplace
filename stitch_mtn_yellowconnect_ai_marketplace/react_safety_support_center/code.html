Verify Caller Identity

MTN Support will never ask for your PIN, OTP, or password over the phone. Always verify official channels before sharing information.

Secure Your Devices

Ensure your merchant device has an active screen lock and avoid logging into Merchant Central on public Wi-Fi networks.

Report Suspicious Links
