Help & Billing Policy

Find answers to common questions and understand our subscription tiers, transaction fees, and refund processes.

live_help Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upgrade to a Platinum Membership? expand_more
You can upgrade your membership tier at any time by navigating to your Profile, selecting 'Membership Plan', and choosing the Platinum option. The prorated difference will be charged to your default payment method immediately.
Are there limits on how many products I can list? expand_more
Gold Merchants can list up to 500 active products simultaneously. Platinum Merchants enjoy unlimited active product listings, along with priority placement in search results.
When are transaction fees deducted? expand_more
Transaction fees are automatically deducted at the point of sale. The net amount is then transferred to your merchant wallet. You can view a detailed breakdown of fees for every order in your Seller Dashboard.

receipt_long Transaction Fees

Our platform charges a processing fee on the total transaction amount, including shipping and taxes.

  • Gold Tier: 2.5% per successful transaction.
  • Platinum Tier: 1.5% per successful transaction.
  • Payment gateway fees (e.g., credit card processing) are independent and typically range from 1.2% to 2.9% + $0.30 depending on the provider and region.

currency_exchange Refund Process

We strive to make the refund process as seamless as possible for both merchants and buyers.

  • Merchants can initiate a full or partial refund directly from the 'Orders' dashboard within 30 days of the transaction.
  • Transaction fees collected by the platform are not refunded when a merchant issues a refund to a customer.
  • Refunds typically take 3-5 business days to appear on the customer's original payment method.
