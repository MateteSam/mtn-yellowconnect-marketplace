Prohibited Activities

Actions that compromise the integrity and safety of the MTN Marketplace ecosystem are strictly forbidden.

Content Standards

  • check_circle All listings must contain accurate, up-to-date information and original photographs.
  • check_circle Communication must remain respectful; hate speech, harassment, or threats will result in immediate suspension.
  • check_circle Do not include personal contact information (phone numbers, emails) directly in public listing descriptions.

Enforcement Actions

Violations of this policy are taken seriously. MTN Marketplace employs AI-driven monitoring and community reporting to enforce these standards.

1st Violation Warning & Listing Removal
2nd Violation Temporary Suspension (7 Days)
Severe/Repeated Permanent Ban
